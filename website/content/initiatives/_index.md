ossbase.org develops and promotes practical initiatives that improve openness, collaboration, and sustainability in digital commons.

Current initiatives include:

- [Open Contributions Descriptor (OCD)](/initiatives/open-contributions-descriptor/) — a machine-readable way for organizations to publish their open source, open data, and open standards activity.
- [CLA Free](/initiatives/cla-free/) — guidance to prefer a Developer Certificate of Origin (DCO) over Contributor License Agreements (CLAs).
